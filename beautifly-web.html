Beautifly — Wedding Cinematography Website

A concept one-page website designed for a wedding cinematography and photography studio. The design creates an elegant, emotional and editorial online presence that helps couples explore featured films, understand the studio’s story and easily get in touch.

Role: UX/UI Designer

Platform: One-page Website

Industry: Wedding Film & Photography

Overview

Beautifly was designed to feel premium, warm and cinematic. The website starts with a strong full-screen hero image, then guides visitors through featured wedding films, the studio’s story and a clear contact section for booking enquiries.

Key Features

  • Full-screen visual hero section for immediate emotional impact
  • Featured Films gallery showcasing wedding footage
  • Elegant editorial layout with warm neutral styling
  • About section focused on trust, story and personalised service
  • Contact section with booking-focused form layout
  • Clean one-page structure for easy visitor flow
