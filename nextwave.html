NextWave — Underground Music Discovery App

A concept music discovery platform designed for underground and emerging artists. NextWave gives artists with under 1,000 plays a fair space to be heard, grow their audience, and earn rewards through Wave Points.

Role: UX/UI Designer

Platform: iOS Mobile App

Project: Concept App

Overview

NextWave flips the traditional streaming model by focusing only on undiscovered artists. Instead of rewarding popularity, the app creates a level playing field where new talent can gain visibility before being buried by algorithms.

Key Features

  • Only artists with under 1,000 plays can be featured
  • Discovery page for rising underground music
  • Artist profiles with plays, followers, tracks and support options
  • Now playing screen with share-based point rewards
  • Wave Points system for plays, shares and follows
  • Artist dashboard with monthly stats and redeemable rewards
