BIO

A creative director with a passion for bridging creativity and culture. As a first generation Liberian American, her heritage is deeply woven into her work, shaping her perspective and inspiring her creative approach.

She takes pride in showcasing cultural narratives with authenticity, confidence, and a strong sense of identity. Her goal is to infuse every project with the same pride and appreciation that she has for where she comes from — creating work that resonates, empowers, and leaves a lasting impact.
