Better Nature — Fitness & Food Tracker

A personalised nutrition tracking app built for the Better Nature tempeh brand, helping users monitor calories, macros, and health goals while discovering plant-based products.

Role: UX/UI Designer

Platform: iOS Mobile App

Brand: Better Nature

Overview

Designed end-to-end from onboarding to core app screens, creating a cohesive experience around Better Nature's brand identity. The app combines food logging with product discovery and personalised goal tracking.

Key Features

  • 6-step personalised onboarding flow with goal setting
  • Daily macro & nutrient tracking with visual progress bars
  • Weekly calendar view with stacked macro charts
  • Integrated product shop tied to nutritional data
  • Food log with quick-add and meal copying
  • Weight tracking and exercise logging
